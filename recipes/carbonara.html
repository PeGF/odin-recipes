Carbonara

A beautiful version of the italian classic Carbonara

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti - 150g;
  • Guanciale - 50g;
  • Pecorino Romano Cheese - 50g;
  • 2 eggs;
  • Freshly ground black pepper.

Steps

  1. Boil the water in a large pan. Don't forget to salt it before putting the pasta
  2. Cook the pasta to al dente
  3. Chop the guanciale in cubes and finely grate the pecorino cheese
  4. Beat 1 whole egg and 1 egg gem. Mix them with the cheese and the black pepper and reserve it
  5. Fry the guanciale until it's golden and crispy and let it rest in the pan
  6. When the pasta is ready, lift it from the cooking water with a pasta fork or tongs and put it in the pan with the guanciale. You will want to put some of the cooking water in the pan aswell
  7. Take the pan out off the heat and let it cool down a bit and then mix the pasta and the guanciale with the eggs and cheese mixture
  8. Add extra pasta water and keep mixing until the eggs mixture becomes a creamy sauce
  9. Twist the pasta on to a serving plate or bowl. Grate extra pecorino and black pepper on top and serve it immediately
