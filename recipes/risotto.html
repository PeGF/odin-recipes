Risotto al Parmigiano

Classic and simple Risotto al Parmigiano

Ingredients

  • Arborio Rice - 400g;
  • Butter - 1/2 stick;
  • Grated parmisan cheese - 1 cup;
  • White wine - 150ml;
  • 1/2 onion;
  • Chicken stock - 1L;
  • Olive oil - 1 tablespoon.

Steps

  1. Heat the pan, add the oliveoil and the sliced onion and let it cook
  2. Add the arborio rice and cook it for about a minute
  3. Pour in the wine and stir until the wine is fully absorbed and there is no more alcool
  4. Add 1/2 cup of the chicken stock and keep stirring until it starts to dry, then add more stock
  5. Repeat the process until the rice is cooked to al dente
  6. finish with cold butter and the grated parmisan cheese, mix everything together ant let it cool down for a minute
  7. Enjoy the classic and simple risotto!
