Lasagna

Easy (but classic) homemade lasagna

Ingredients

  • 12 lasagna pasta sheets;
  • 1 pound of ground beef;
  • 2 cans of San Marzano Tomatoes;
  • 1 large onion;
  • 1 carrot;
  • 3 cups of shredded mozzarella cheese;
  • 1/2 cup of grated parmesan cheese;
  • salt and black pepper to taste;
  • Olive oil.

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180 degrees C (350 degrees F)
  2. Heat a big pot over medium heat. Add the olive oil, the sliced onion and the sliced carrot to the pot. Cook it until the bottom of the pan starts to become brown.
  3. Add the ground beef to the pot and cook it for about 10 minutes
  4. Add the tomatoes, salt and black pepper and let it simmer for about 30 minutes
  5. Spread 4 tablespoons of bolognese sauce in a retangular baking dish
  6. Cover with 4 lasagna pasta sheets
  7. Add 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese and 1 cup of bolognese sauce
  8. Repeat the process twice
  9. Cover the last layer with the parmesan cheese
  10. Bake the lasagna in the preheated oven for about 45 minutes. Let it stand 10 minutes before slicing and serving
